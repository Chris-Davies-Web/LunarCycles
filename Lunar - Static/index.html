Lunar Cycles

A bicycle workshop in Kentish Town offering a full range of servicing, repairs and refurbishments.

How we work

Want to watch what we do? We'll happily walk you through your repair.

Or let us do the work and grab a coffee from the terrific The Fields Beneath while you wait.

We'll text you when your bike is ready to collect.

We can lend you a courtesy bike if yours has to stay overnight.
